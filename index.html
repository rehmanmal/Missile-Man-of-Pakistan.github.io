Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan

1936 - 2021

Missile Man of Pakistan

Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan was born in Bhopal, India, in 1936 and later migrated to Pakistan. He played a significant role in Pakistan's nuclear program in the 1970s and 1980s, helping Pakistan develop its first nuclear weapons. His work led to Pakistan's successful nuclear tests in 1998.The "Missile Man of Pakistan" is a title often associated with Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan. Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan is a Pakistani scientist and engineer who is known for his role in Pakistan's nuclear weapons program and missile development. He is considered a key figure in Pakistan's efforts to acquire nuclear weapons technology.
